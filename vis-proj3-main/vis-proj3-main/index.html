"South Park" is an animated television series created by Trey Parker and Matt Stone that premiered in 1997. Known for its satirical and often controversial humor, the show revolves around four boys—Eric Cartman, Stan Marsh, Kyle Broflovski, and Kenny McCormick (as respectively shown below)—and their bizarre adventures in the small town of South Park, Colorado. "South Park" is famous for its crude animation style, dark comedy, and its approach to tackling a wide range of topics, often addressing social issues, celebrity culture, and current events with a distinctively disrespectful tone. The series employs a fast production schedule, allowing it to remain particularly topical by addressing issues that are very current, making it a unique blend of comedy and commentary. There are currently 26 seasons (327 episodes) of "South Park" with almost no end in sight even with the numerous times it has been target by "cancel culture."
